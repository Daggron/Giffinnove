Our Inventions

Here are some of our inventions we did at gff innovations

Moksh
GFF Innovations - "MOKSH" a machine which traverses an agriculture farm post-harvest of a rice crop. It picks the rice straw from the ground on a real-time basis and converts the rice-straw into a powder form.
e-DeWeeder
The unwanted elements in any way has always led to troubles and unnecessary consequences. Similarly with new advancements and changing soil conditions with the crops, the gold of earth comes the unwanted weeds. E- Deweeder the one which saves the manual load of a farmer and elimates weeds using sensors.
e-Pancreas
Why be worried about your insulin level every time when you can let e-pancreas do the work for you. E-pancreas a wearable non-invasive continuous glucose monitoring device which lets you know your insulin level anytime you want. With an automated insulin delivery system which works painlessly.
BhoGoal - Predicting Moods of INDIA!
With its accuracy and precision, who would need the skies to turn gray on sunny days to predict rains? Bhugol, with a revolutionary technology tells you the exact time and date of the incoming of rains. Saving the crops, farmers and the layman from the wrath of thunders!
The Pied Piper
Running behind rats and rodents brings a pain in the back. Hard to catch and leaves behinds harmful bacteria as well.
